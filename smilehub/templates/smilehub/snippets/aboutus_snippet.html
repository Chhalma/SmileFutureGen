"Smile for Future Generation" is situated in the Ilemela district, Mwanza City Council, specifically in Magaka village.
Recognizing the essential role of the education sector in realizing the country's vision of industrialization and economic development, and consequently improving the quality of life, the organization aims to contribute to this vision. Despite the government of Tanzania's efforts to enhance education services by providing free education in primary and secondary schools, there remain marginalized and vulnerable children who do not fully benefit from this opportunity.

To address this gap, it is crucial for non-state actors to collaborate and complement the government's efforts in improving educational services for children, particularly those from vulnerable families, especially in rural areas where many are disadvantaged. This collaborative effort will involve implementing innovative ideas tailored to respective communities, ensuring equitable access to education services for children aged 6 to 18 years old.
